I am a knowledge seeker and after I parted ways with customer service, I decided to take the time to explore knowledge seeking and researching on my own. Because of this, I was able to teach my self many technical skills including responsive web design, cloud computing, and data analytics. This led me to my current apprenticeship with HP.
